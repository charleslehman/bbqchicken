Sibi Smart Jobs

Key Features

1. Job Creation

Problem: Property Management orgs often struggle with managing and tracking maintenance and repair requests from tenants.

Proposed Solution: By providing an easy way to create jobs when tenants report issues, our system helps property managers stay organized and ensures that no request falls through the cracks.

2. Property Based Rules

Problem: Property managers may have difficulty ensuring certain organization rules/compliance when their pros/vendors are purchasing products and materials for maintenance and repairs.

Proposed Solution: By setting up property-based rules, our system automatically enforces compliance, reducing the risk of errors and saving time for property managers.

3. Smart Shop for Materials

Problem: Selecting the appropriate materials for a job can be time-consuming and requires extensive knowledge of the property and its requirements.

Proposed Solution: Our smart shop feature simplifies the material selection process by providing a curated catalog based on the job and property rules, saving time and ensuring accuracy.

4. Draft Jobs

Problem: Collaboration and approval processes can be cumbersome and lead to delays in executing jobs.

Proposed Solution: The ability to create and modify draft jobs allows for seamless collaboration and streamlines the approval process, ultimately reducing the time it takes to complete maintenance and repair tasks.

5. Order Placement

Problem: Manually placing orders for materials and services can be time-consuming and prone to errors.

Proposed Solution: By automatically placing orders upon the execution of a job, our system saves time for property managers and reduces the risk of errors in the ordering process.
